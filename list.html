List Your Property on H2H

Turn your extra space into income! Listing on Home-to-Home is simple:

  1. Contact the H2H team via phone +1 (551) 579-4132 or email ceo@asaanpay.shop.
  2. Take clear, well-lit photos of every room and any shared spaces.
  3. Write a short description: number of beds, bathrooms, amenities (Wi-Fi, kitchen, parking, pool, etc.).
  4. Set your nightly price and availability calendar.
  5. Send everything to us — we’ll create your listing and publish it within 24 hours.

That’s it! Once live, guests can book instantly and you earn every night.
